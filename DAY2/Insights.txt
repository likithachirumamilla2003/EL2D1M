Technology is the top-performing category, contributing the highest total sales compared to Furniture and Office Supplies. This shows strong customer demand for tech products.

The Consumer segment generates the highest sales overall, outperforming Corporate and Home Office segments, making it the primary revenue driver.

The West region contributes the highest share of total sales, indicating better market penetration and customer activity compared to other regions.

Home Office segment consistently shows lower sales across regions, suggesting either limited demand or fewer orders from small/home-based businesses.

Certain regions like Central Asia and Africa show comparatively lower sales, indicating potential underperforming markets or opportunities for business expansion.
